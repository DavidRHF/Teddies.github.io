Our Story

A family tradition of warmth, comfort, and woodland hospitality.

How Teddies Began

Teddies started as a small wooden cabin tucked away in Evergreen Valley. The founder, Theodore “Teddy” Bennett, believed food should feel like a warm hug.

What began as a simple kitchen serving hikers and travelers soon became a beloved gathering place for families across the valley.

Today, we still cook with the same philosophy: simple ingredients, slow cooking, and meals made with heart.
